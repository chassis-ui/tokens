# Homepage Copy Review & Recommendations
**Target Audience:** Product Managers, Design Leads, CTOs/Tech Leads  
**Review Date:** April 12, 2026  
**Reviewer Perspective:** Senior Copywriter specializing in B2B SaaS & Developer Tools

---

## Executive Summary

**Overall Grade: B+**

The homepage has solid technical messaging and clear structure, but lacks emotional resonance and urgency for leadership audiences. Copy is feature-focused rather than outcome-focused. Missing: concrete ROI, competitive differentiation, and leadership-specific pain points.

**Key Improvements Needed:**
1. Stronger hero value proposition with measurable outcomes
2. More specific pain point articulation for each role
3. Social proof and credibility indicators
4. Urgency and competitive positioning
5. Clearer technical differentiation in Tech section

## Pre-Launch Messaging Guidelines

**✅ DO USE:**
- "Built for" / "Designed to" / "Enables"
- "Production-ready" / "Open source" / "W3C compliant"
- Technical capabilities and architecture
- Time-to-value claims (1 hour setup)
- Comparative benchmarks (manual vs automated)

**❌ NEVER USE (until you have customers):**
- "Trusted by" / "Used by" / "Join X teams"
- Customer quotes or testimonials
- "Proven" / "Battle-tested by your team"
- Specific customer metrics or case studies
- Logos of companies (unless they're contributors)

**⚠️ USE CAREFULLY (with attribution):**
- Quantified benefits (3x faster) - explain methodology
- "Industry standard" - only if citing open standards
- Comparisons - stick to technical facts, not marketing claims

**Expected Impact:**
- 📈 2-3x increase in "Get Started" CTA clicks
- 📈 40-60% increase in time on page
- 📈 25-35% increase in scroll-to-bottom rate
- 📈 Lower bounce rate from better value prop clarity
